Services

PCS is a simple standard that can be used for the Validation and verification of afforestation and reforestation, and renewable energy projects, described in simple language enabling anyone to understand it. We increase awareness by conducting training on the reduction of carbon emissions through lean processing, reduction of carbon footprint through education, carbon footprint calculations using the PCS, and developing strategies and policies to promote a low carbon economy.

Certify a project

Verification and validation of carbon projects registered with PCS are done by an independent 3rd Party. Sri Lanka Climate Fund acts as the verification and Validation body for PCS projects in Sri Lanka and other countries. SLCF can issue Sri Lanka-certified Emission reductions (SLCER).
